Hi, we have three guys, Zhiyi Wang, Dongsheng Li and Juntong Liu. The project of us is to make a microprocessor controlled toy car. The first part is to assemble the car, we uses a generic smart car chasis to build it. In regard to the hardware side, we choose Arduino nano to controll it. In addition, we have bluetooth, ultrasonic and speed sensor to controll it. Hopefully, we can use cell phones to controll it. Most of the code will be updated on github.
